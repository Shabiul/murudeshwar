Journey Into Diving
Akhildev discovered diving in 2024 while visiting Kochi for a short break. What began as a single try-dive transformed into an obsession — the silence, the colors, and the slow ballet of reef life hooked him immediately.

Q&A
Q: What made you stay?

"The moment you take that first breath underwater, everything else stops. I wanted to keep feeling that peace — and then I wanted to share it with others."

Training & Growth
Akhildev pushed through his certifications quickly because he asked the right questions and practiced deliberately. He combined formal training with hands-on shifts on the boat, learning how to calm groups, set up kit, and manage pre-dive nerves.

Q&A
Q: How do you learn so fast?

"I break a skill into small parts, drill each part until it's natural, then put it back together in real conditions. Also — I never stop asking why."

Most Memorable Dive
One sunrise dive with a pod of dolphins and a passing manta near a shallow reef cemented his love for conservation storytelling.

"The dolphins came within arm's length, moving like a single organism. It felt cinematic — a moment I still edit into short clips for our conservation stories."
