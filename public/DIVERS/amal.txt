Journey Into Diving
Godwin's childhood boat trips left him wondering how fish live when they're not on the line. Introduced to scuba by his brother, he discovered a vocation: to meet marine life as a witness — not a taker.

Q&A
Q: What drew you most to guiding others?

"Seeing someone go from terror to wonder — that transformation is my reward."

Training & Growth
Godwin earned core certifications including Deep Diver specialty and focused heavily on stress-management techniques for beginners. He pairs practical drills with breathing exercises and positive reinforcement.

"I always start with: 'What scares you most?' Then we dismantle that fear in small pieces."

Most Memorable Dive
Early deep dives in Trivandrum — stark, mysterious, and full of surprising life forms — taught him to slow down and observe.

"In the dark, everything is amplified. That's where I learned patience."

Behind the Scenes — What Guests Don't See
Godwin invests time in one-on-one encouragement before the boat departs, sometimes spending 15–20 minutes walking a nervous diver through each step until they feel ready.

"A little time spent before the dive saves a lot of stress during it."

Team Experience at Scuba Spirit
He's the emotional anchor — if a student freezes, the team calls Godwin. He's also active in storytelling, sharing local fishing lore to build respect between visitors and coastal communities.
